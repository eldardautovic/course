Greetings and salutations, defenders!

Another threat rises above the horizon, a threat so great that we have gathered only the greatest of minds to help us combat it.

The threat is.. Bugs.

We need you!

We are organizing the defense in the city of Mostar, far in the south of Bosnia and Herzegovina where the sun always shines as to help us identify the dreaded bugs, of course. We will fight them and learn their tricks for 10 days, observing how they behave and how we can find them. The days will be filled with new experiences and grave danger (Not really) that we are sure you will be able to overcome!

A new form of life based on two mixed magic spells called Automated and Manual testing can enlighten your vision of serving for this purpose. You can express power of mighty Quality Assurance developer, but are you willing to take a risk? So many secrets separate you now, but you’re just one application away from those secrets. Prepare for challenges that help you develop, income for the higher rank and relaxed environment are waiting.

You can bet on it that you will learn about an astonishing topic and get motivation for a bigger picture of an IT world. Meet the MostarAvengers and gather the knowledge in any way. Spend the mysterious days and warm nights in the company of BESTies under the Old Bridge with wild vision of life. Enjoy the Bosnian food and culture every day and every night.

Avengers, assemble!
